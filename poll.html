Price Discrimination Types:

First Degree

Charging each customer their maximum willingness to pay (perfect discrimination)

Second Degree

Price varies by quantity or version (quantity discounts, premium versions)

Third Degree

Different prices for identifiable groups (student discounts, senior rates)
