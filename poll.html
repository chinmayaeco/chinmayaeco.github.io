Key Business Insights

  • Government spending multipliers are typically larger than tax multipliers
  • Infrastructure spending often has high multipliers due to job creation
  • Tax cuts for lower-income households have higher multipliers
  • Multipliers are higher during recessions than expansions
  • Timing lags can reduce multiplier effectiveness
