Price Elasticity of Demand

Shows how quantity demanded responds to price changes

Current: 0%
Current: 1.0 (Elastic)

Interpretation:

Elastic Demand (|PED| > 1): Quantity changes more than price (luxury goods)

Unitary Elastic (|PED| = 1): Quantity changes equal to price change

Inelastic Demand (|PED| < 1): Quantity changes less than price (necessities)
