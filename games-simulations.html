Simulation Panel

Make decisions in this simulated business environment.

Fixed Costs: Costs that do not change with the level of production (e.g., rent, salaries).
Variable Costs: Costs that vary directly with production volume (e.g., raw materials).
Sunk Costs: Costs that have already been incurred and cannot be recovered.
Economic Costs: The total opportunity costs of production, including both explicit and implicit costs.
Accounting Costs: The actual out-of-pocket expenses recorded in financial statements.
