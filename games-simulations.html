IS Curve

Consumption

Key Business Insights

  • Steeper IS curves mean investment is less sensitive to interest rates
  • IS shifts indicate changes in autonomous spending components
  • Fiscal policy directly shifts the IS curve
  • Monetary policy causes movements along the IS curve
  • Expectations can cause significant IS curve shifts
