Key Financial Insights

  • Flatter LM curves mean money demand is more sensitive to interest rates
  • Money supply changes directly shift the LM curve
  • Income changes cause movements along the LM curve
  • Liquidity traps make monetary policy less effective
  • Expectations can influence money demand significantly
